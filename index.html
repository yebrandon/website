Red and Blue

Feb.2018 - Mar. 2018
Unity, C#

A narrative-driven 2D side scrolling RPG accompanied by a short documentary about the creative process in game development. Not for a hackathon, not for a club, not for my resume, but for my highschool english class. I'd loved video games throughout my entire life, and dreamt of one day making one myself, so when the opportunity came I knew I wanted to go for it. Looking back years later, Red and Blue still has to be my favourite personal project - I learned so many things about programming, about storytelling, about myself - it was the project that made me realize I could create amazing things.

Tax Simulator

Nov. 2019
Unity, C#

An educational game geared towards teaching youth about tax deductions and savings vehicles, developed with a team of 4 in 36 hours at Hack Western VI in London, ON, and awarded Best Education Hack to Introduce Students to Taxes and Benefits. Smart Windows

Mar. 2020
React, Javascript, Flask, Python, Raspberry Pi

A proof of concept IoT system that detects environmental information about windows and allows you to control them, built in 42 hours with a team of 6 at the L-Spark Secure IoT Hackathon in Kingston, ON and awarded Best Overall Implementation of Secure IoT Dev Kit. This was a fun one. We had a ton of other project ideas for this hackathon at first, but alas, potential masterpieces like "Uber Watermelon" and "literally a smoke detector" weren't able to make the cut. I mostly worked on building the front-end, but getting see things come togheter work with the Raspberry Pi and moisture detection hardware was really cool. Also the food there was great!
